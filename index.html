HOW TO PLAY

Rummy is a simple card game that is played with two or more players. It requires 2 decks of cards, if the number of players exceeds five. First player at a table arranges the cards which are dealt into predefined combinations and submits the melded cards. Your primary aim is to be the first players as the rest of the players accumulate points based on the cards in their hands. The player who has the least number of points at the end of a game wins the game.

  • Pool Rummy

    • In this variation of 13 card rummy, players pool in their buy-in value and this is set as the total value of the table. The player who wins this online rummy game takes home the entire amount at the table. This again has two modes of play, which are the 101 pool rummy and the 201 pool rummy. In the former mode, the player who reaches 101 points first, is eliminated from the rummy table. Similarly, in the latter version, this elimination threshold is 201 points. This makes pool rummy a highly competitive form of 13 card rummy game since all the players at the table are focused on indirectly eliminating the other by using cunning strategies.

  • Deals Rummy

    • This version of 13 card rummy begins with a decided number of rounds or deals, and at the end of which, the player with the highest number of chips wins the rummy game. For example, deals rummy can be played with a best of 2 or best of 3 deals, and at the end of the 2nd or 3rd deal respectively, the player with the highest number of chips, wins. In each deal, the winner receives chips from other players based on the value of cards in the hands of the losers. Finally, the player with the maximum number of chips among the other players wins the online rummy game.

  • Points Rummy

    • This is yet another highly popular and sought-after variant of 13 card rummy that most players love to play online. In points rummy, players play for a pre-decided value of points and the sum of all the points of the players at the table make up the total points. However, the player with zero points is ultimately declared the winner of the rummy game.
