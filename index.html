How to Play

  • Objective of Under10: Be the player with the score of less than 10
  • Number of players: 2 or more
  • Number of cards: 52 card deck game and 4 cards dealt to each player
  • Rank of cards: Ace (low) – King (high) and value of number cards is same as printed like value of card 2 of any suit is 2.
  • Value of Ace is 1 and King is of 13
  • Under10 is a card game played with 4 hand cards. Players will have to pick and discard card from open/close deck to make the sum of cards in hand value less than 10.
  • On player turn, firstly have to discard a card then pick from open or closed deck. Player can discard multiple cards of same value.
  • Players earn points based on the cards pick and discard. The player who ended the hand with less than 10 points, will get the declare button. The player with lowest sum of cards (Under10) wins.

Prize Money Scenarios

  • 1st Position Player (winner) = 2 x entry fee - commission
  • Other Players = entry fee - commission
  • Last position Player = 0
  • If there are 2 winners, rank will be decided based on the declaration sequence
  • If there are 2 last position players, then both will get 0
  • If more than 2 players and playing any more than 1 player leave the game at mid, then winner will get other players amount as bonus
