Fair Play

Rummy game is a individual's skill not a team effort. Hence, as part of our Fair Play Policy the following aspects covered are to safeguard the true spirit of skill & competition in online rummy at The Under 10.

Your Chat Privileges

Certain forms and topics of chat are not allowed on The Under 10. Chat privileges of players who are engaging in offensive chat will be subject to a suspension. .

Profanity/Abuse

The Under 10 prohibits profane, vulgar, racist or abusive/insulting chat in our chat windows.

Begging/Solicitation

The Under 10 prohibits players to beg for chips , whether play money or real money. No solicitation is allowed in any rummy at any time.

Chip Sales

Players are not permitted to engage in any discussion regarding the sale of The Under 10 chips within our chat facilities. Any such discussion, including the offer to add or sell chips in The Under 10 chat, is grounds for suspension.

Flooding

The sending of multiple and frequent messages to the chat window in order to drown out legitimate chat is not allowed.

Commercial Use

It is strictly prohibited for a User to use Chat for any commercial use whatsoever, including making any statements which promote any service or product of any party except The Under 10.

Malicious Behaviour

It is strictly prohibited for a User to use Chat for any malicious or offensive behaviour, including collusion, fraud, spam, and/or harassment. Users shall not make statements about The Under 10 that are untrue or would reasonably be considered to be derogatory or critical .

Live Chat

The Under 10 does not actively monitor chat , and you play at tables with chat enabled at your own risk. You may not make any chat comments which may affect the play of any hand or tournament.

Report a Problem

We encourage players to help us be our eyes and ears. The cooperation of our players is a critical part of making The Under 10 a fun experience for all. Please do not engage in retaliatory chat; as this will likely result in both users chat being suspended . Please report any of the above offenses to support@rummyroom.com

Fraud & Collusion Protection

Games on The Under 10 are monitored round the clock to detect any fraudulent behavior, Anti-Collusion measures are undertaken before, during and after each game on The Under 10.

Before Game Starts Seating is random, and no seat is prefixed for any game. Players, therefore have no control over selection of players on any table on our platform.

To shuffle cards, the Random Number Generator used by The Under 10 has been certified by iTech Labs, an IEC/ISO 17025 accredited testing laboratory.

Players logged in from the same IP address cannot be allocated seats on the same table.

In-Play

On The Under 10, information about the playing cards is always encrypted, thereby preventing any third party from viewing the same.

Our Anti-fraud algorithms track every move (selection of cards, discards, melds, shows) by every player on every table round the clock.

After Game ends

Players suspected of collusion are blocked from playing at the same table in future.
